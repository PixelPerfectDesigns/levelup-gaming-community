Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join a tournament?

Create an account, visit our Events page, and register for any open tournaments. Most tournaments have specific requirements and registration deadlines.

Is LevelUp Gaming free to join?

Yes! Creating an account and joining our community is completely free. Some premium tournaments may have entry fees, but most of our events are free to participate in.

What games do you support?

We support a wide variety of games including battle royales, FPS games, strategy games, and more. Check our Games page for the complete list of supported titles.

How do I report a problem?

You can report issues through our Discord server, email us at support@levelupgaming.com, or use the contact form above. Our support team typically responds within 24 hours.

Can I organize my own tournament?

Absolutely! Community members can request to host their own tournaments. Contact us with your tournament idea and we'll help you get it set up.

Do you offer coaching services?

Yes! We have a community coaching program where experienced players mentor newcomers. Check our Community page for more information about finding a coach.
